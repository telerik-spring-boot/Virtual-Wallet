Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions related to your virtual wallet experience. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us.

To verify your email, simply follow the instructions sent to your email after registration. Once verified, you will need to submit your ID and a selfie for admin verification. After approval, you'll be able to make transactions.

You can transfer funds to other users by using their IBAN, username, or phone number. Large transfers will require email confirmation for security purposes.

Yes, you can buy stocks directly through the app, provided you have sufficient funds in your wallet. You can use the funds from your wallet to purchase stocks.

If you forget your password, you can easily reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password securely.

Yes, you can hold multiple currencies in your wallet. However, your main wallet will always be in USD, and other currencies will be added as secondary wallets.

Joint wallets allow multiple users to share access to a single wallet. The creator of the joint wallet is the admin and has the authority to add or remove members from the wallet.

To deposit money into your wallet, navigate to the deposit section of the app. You can use various payment methods like bank transfer, credit card, or other supported payment systems.

You can view your transaction history by going to the "Transactions" section in the app. Here, you will see a list of all your past transfers, deposits, and investments, along with their details.

If you forget to confirm a large transfer via email, the transaction will not be processed.

To delete your account, you can contact our support team through the app's contact form. They will assist you with the process, including securely closing your account and handling any remaining balance.
